Welcome to the Memetic Vault

This is an experiment in tracking how ideas and images spread through direct sharing.

How it works:

  • A "vault" starts with a single image and grows as people add their own.
  • Each contribution creates a unique link that can be shared with the next person.
  • We trace the lineage of contributions to see how the vault evolves and travels.
