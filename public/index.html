Message in a Bottle

Create a digital message in a bottle, fill it with a meaningful image and your thoughts, and send it out on a journey across the internet! See how far it travels and what unique stories and cultural snapshots it collects along the way.

How it Works: The Voyage

Step 1: Launch Your Bottle

Start by choosing an image that resonates with you. Set a creative prompt for future contributors and add a few survey questions to guide the bottle's theme. Answer your own questions first!

Step 2: Share the Link

Once created, you'll get a unique secret link â€“ like the map coordinates for your bottle. Share this link directly with a friend you trust to continue the journey.

Step 3: Add to the Story & Pass It On

Your friend uses the link, sees the bottle's history (including your contribution!), adds their own image based on your prompt, and answers the survey questions. They then receive a *new* unique link to pass along to someone else.

Step 4: Watch it Drift

The bottle continues to travel from person to person, gathering images, answers, and moments. Each one becomes a unique time capsule, a snapshot of the connections and cultures it passes through.

A Digital Artifact

Each completed journey is a collaborative piece of art, a miniature cultural survey, and a testament to shared human experience across digital shores.
